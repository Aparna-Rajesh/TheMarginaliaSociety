What is it?

The Marginalia Society is a series of gatherings modeled loosely on college seminars and old-school literary salons. Each session will feature one big question, a few shared readings, and a room of people who like to talk things through.

How does it work?

Prior to each meeting, I’ll share a document with one thematic question to drive our discussion and a handful of short required readings so we start on the same page.

The document will also have a space where participants can share other related material they found interesting.

How should you prepare?
  1. Read the required pieces at some point before we meet.
  2. Bring your thoughts and a snack or bottle to share :)

See you there!
